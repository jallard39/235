Layout Sketches

Phase 1: spec.html

Goal

I plan to do a personal portfolio for this project (or at least the foundations of one). I hope to create a base for a portfolio that I can add to later. For now, the content will consist primarily of some self-introduction and the group game project from IGME 106. The first part of the website will be a professional introduction (essentially a cover letter) and some personal photos. The second part will include an overview of the 106 group game, my specific contributions (possibly divided into 3-4 subsections/side panels), and some takeaways from the project, along with screenshots or (potentially) videos.

Notes

  • Initially I tried to use named grid areas (grid-template-areas) to place the items into the grid layout, but for some reason it wasn't working. I ended up just brute-forcing it with grid lines and span.

Phase 2: index.html

Notes

  • HOLY CRAP THIS TOOK ME FOREVER. Web development is a lot more time-consuming than I thought.
  • I'm still not entirely happy with everything (especially navigation and color scheme) but I feel that it is at a point where the site is at least useable. I plan to go back and iron out the kinks before using it professionally.
  • I am pretty proud of the image grids. They were one of the hardest parts of the page to get right, but I am relatively satisfied with how they are currently. They are also responsive!
